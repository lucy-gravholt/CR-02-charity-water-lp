The Water Crisis

Clean Water Changes Everything

Right now, millions of people around the world wake up every day without access to clean water. It’s a crisis—but one we can solve.

Without clean water, children miss school, families fall ill, and communities struggle to grow. But when clean water flows, hope begins to rise. Health improves. Time is reclaimed. Women lead. Children learn. And communities thrive.

This crisis is urgent—but it’s also incredibly solvable. With your help, we can bring lasting change to people who need it most

Our Impact

Together, We're Changing Lives

Thanks to generous supporters like you, Charity: Water has funded over 186,000 water projects in 29 countries, bringing clean water to more than 20 million people—and counting.

Every well, every tap, every system we build together is a symbol of hope and opportunity. Clean water unlocks education, health, economic growth, and dignity.

Your donations don’t just fund projects—they fuel stories of transformation. And because 100% of public donations go directly to water solutions, you can trust your generosity is making a real difference.
